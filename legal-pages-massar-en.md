At **Massar Agency**, we place great importance on protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what your rights are as a user of our website and services (web development, app development, brand identity).

Processing of your data is based on:
- Your **consent** (forms, newsletter)

Massar Agency does not sell or rent your personal data to third parties. Your data may only be shared with:
- Our technical service providers (hosting, analytics tools), under a contractual confidentiality obligation
- Competent authorities, where required by law

### 1.5 Retention Period

Your data is retained for:
- **3 years** after the last contact for prospects
- **Duration of the contractual relationship + 5 years** for clients (accounting and legal obligations)
- **13 months maximum** for analytics cookies

### 1.6 Your Rights

In accordance with Algerian legislation on the protection of personal data (Law No. 18-07), you have the following rights:

- Right of access to your data
- Right to rectification
- Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")

### 1.7 Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures (encryption, restricted access, backups) to protect your data against unauthorized access, loss, or disclosure.

## 3. Cookie Policy

### 3.1 What Is a Cookie?

A cookie is a small text file placed on your device (computer, tablet, smartphone) while browsing our site. It allows us to collect information related to your browsing and provide you with a personalized experience.

### 3.2 Why Do We Use Cookies?

Massar Agency uses cookies to:

- **Ensure the proper functioning of the site** (essential cookies)
- **Analyze audience and visitor behavior** (Google Analytics 4, Core Web Vitals measurement)
- **Remember your preferences** (language, display settings)
- **Improve our SEO and technical performance**

### 3.3 Types of Cookies Used

| Cookie Type | Purpose | Retention Period |
|---|---|---|
| **Essential cookies** | Technical operation of the site (navigation, security) | Session |
| **Analytics cookies** | Traffic statistics, user behavior (GA4) | Up to 13 months |
| **Preference cookies** | Remembering your choices (language, display) | Up to 12 months |
| **Third-party cookies** | External integrations (social media, embedded videos) | Varies by provider |

### 3.4 Consent and Cookie Management

On your first visit, a banner informs you of the use of cookies and allows you to:

- Accept all cookies
- Refuse non-essential cookies
- Customize your preferences by category

You can change your preferences at any time via your browser settings or the cookie management module on our site.

### 3.5 How to Disable Cookies via Your Browser

- **Chrome**: Settings > Privacy and security > Cookies
- **Firefox**: Settings > Privacy & Security
- **Safari**: Preferences > Privacy
- **Edge**: Settings > Cookies and site permissions

⚠️ Disabling certain cookies may affect the proper functioning of the site.

### 3.6 Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies come from third-party services integrated into our site (Google Analytics, social media). These third parties have their own privacy policies, which we encourage you to review.

### 3.7 Policy Updates

This cookie policy may be updated to reflect legal, technical, or practice changes. We encourage you to review it regularly.

### 3.8 Contact

For any questions regarding our use of cookies, contact us via our **Contact** page.
